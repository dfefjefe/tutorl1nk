Your Questions, Answered

Find answers to the most commonly asked questions about TutorL1nk.

How do I schedule a session?

You can book sessions through our website, contact us via email, or call us directly. Flexible time slots are available for your convenience.

Do you offer online tutoring?

Yes! We provide interactive online sessions through platforms like Zoom, equipped with tools such as virtual whiteboards and screen sharing.

What subjects do you teach?

We specialize in Mathematics, Science, English, History, and Computer Science for all grade levels, plus test preparation services.

What is your cancellation policy?

You may cancel or reschedule up to 24 hours before the session. Late cancellations may incur a fee.

Are your tutors certified?

All our tutors are experienced professionals with relevant certifications and a passion for teaching.
